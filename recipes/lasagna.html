Lasagna Recipes

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.
It's a hearty meal that's perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmessan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles as per package instruction.
  3. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce, add salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella, Repeat.
  7. Top with parmessan.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before seving
