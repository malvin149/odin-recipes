Pancakes Recipes

Description

Fluffy pancakes are a breakfast favorite.
Easy to make and perfect with syrup, butter or fruits.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter(Melted)
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder,sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingrdients. Stir until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles on the surface.
  7. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  8. Repeat with remaining batter.
  9. Serve with your favorite toppings.
