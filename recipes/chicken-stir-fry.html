Chicken Stir-Fry Recipes

Description

Chicken stir-fry is a quick and healthy dish full of colorful vegetables and tender chicken in a savory sauce.
Great for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers(red, green, yellow)
  • Green onions
  • Broccoli
  • Carrot
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Pepper
  • Cornstarch
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice chicken and vegetables.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, cornstarch,garlic, ginger, and water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  4. Cook chicken until no longer pink. Remove and set aside.
  5. Stir-fry vegetables until tender-crisp.
  6. Return chicken to the pan.
  7. Pour in the sauce and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
